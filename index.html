Plan snapshot

  • Eligibility: Age 21 — immediate entry
  • Safe Harbor: 3% non-elective employer contribution
  • Vesting: 0% <1yr • 33% 1–2yrs • 66% 2–3yrs • 100% 3+yrs
  • Roth: Available
  • Loans: Allowed (standard loan rules)

How to enroll

  1. Click Access Plan Portal and log in (or create an account).
  2. Choose Traditional (pre-tax) or Roth. Set payroll deferral %.
  3. Consider contributing at least 3% (you still receive the safe-harbor 3%).
