Enrollment & Contributions

To enroll: use the portal, pick a contribution percentage (pre‑tax or Roth), and select investments. Consider contributing at least 3% to receive full safe harbor credit (you receive the 3% regardless of your contribution).

  • Step 1: Click "Open Portal" and log in (or create an account).
  • Step 2: Choose Traditional (pre‑tax) or Roth (after‑tax) contributions.
  • Step 3: Set payroll contribution percent.
  • Step 4: Select investments or use the default target date fund.
2025 contribution limit examples: $23,500 standard; $7,500 catch-up (50+). Confirm current limits on your portal or the IRS website.

Employer Match & Vesting

Safe Harbor non‑elective: 3% of pay credited for eligible participants (paid by Mammini regardless of your deferral). Employer match rules and additional non‑elective contributions, if any, are described in the plan documents.

Vesting (employer contributions): 0% under 1 year; 33% at 1–2 yrs; 66% at 2–3 yrs; 100% at 3+ yrs.

FAQs

How do I enroll?
Enroll via the participant portal. Need help? Call (858) 444‑9750 or email info@mammini.com.
What is the safe harbor contribution?
Mammini provides a 3% non‑elective safe harbor contribution to eligible employees each Plan Year (you receive it whether you defer or not).
Are loans allowed?
Yes. The plan permits loans subject to the Loan Procedures. Contact the plan administrator for details.
Can I contribute to a Roth?
Yes — Roth 401(k) contributions are available. Consult a tax advisor for implications.
