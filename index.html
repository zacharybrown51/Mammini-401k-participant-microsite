Access Your 401(k) Account

Plan Basics

  • Eligibility: Age 21
  • Entry: Immediate
  • Safe Harbor Contribution: 3% non‑elective, whether you contribute or not
  • Vesting: 0% <1 yr • 33% (1–2 yrs) • 66% (2–3 yrs) • 100% (3+ yrs)
  • Roth: Available
  • Loans: Allowed (standard rules)
  • Auto Features: None — no auto‑enrollment or auto‑increase

How to Enroll & Get Started

  1. Click "Access Your 401(k) Account" above.
  2. Create your login if you’re a first‑time user.
  3. Choose Traditional (pre‑tax) or Roth deferrals.
  4. Select your payroll contribution %.
  5. Choose investments from the available lineup.

Investments

Your plan includes a diversified set of investment options designed for long‑term savings:

  • Target Date Funds
  • Equity (Stock) Funds
  • Bond Funds
  • Capital Preservation Options

To review performance, allocations, or make changes, log in through the plan portal anytime.

FAQs

Can I take a loan?

Yes. Loans are available under standard plan and IRS rules. Log in to review the terms.

Does the company contribute even if I don’t?

Yes — the 3% safe‑harbor contribution is automatic for all eligible employees.

How do I reset my password?

Go to the login page and select “Forgot Password.” The portal will guide you through it.
